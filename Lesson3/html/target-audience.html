Target Audience

The target audience for this website is nearly everyone who has access to the internet, and either lives in one of the areas covered or are going to or through them. If a person wants to know the commerce in/around one of those areas (Southeast Region of Brazil), they are the targets for this website.

Persona 1:

Persona:

Hotel Owner in São Paulo, Brazil

Fictional Name:

Doug Press

Job Title:

Hotel Owner

Demographics:

  • 31 years old
  • Married
  • 2 children
  • Went to school for a degree in Information Technology, but decided to run his own business

Goals and Tasks:

Grow Membership for his hotel by:

  • Advertising in different websites
  • Getting good reviews on it

Environment:

Between his job and his school, he doesn't have much time. With his major being IT he knows how to operate a computer and websites. Though, because he doesn't have much time, he likes websites to be simple and easy to navigate, especially when those websites will help him keep people interested on his business.

Quote:

"My hotel is one of the most important things to me now. I wish I could make it easier to people know it."

Scenarios:

  • Doug started a new membership program in his hotel. People can get discounts and special promotions, he needs to advertise that as soon as possible, so he can get new member quickly.
  • He is searching for new business partners, so the services at the hotel can be improved. For it happens, he needs to increase his income profit, so he asks for good reviews about his hotel.
  • Doug, as a business owner, likes things that makes people's lives easier and faster. He is searching for a place to recommend his hotel where people can find it easier and have all the information they need to book a room or start a membership.
  • He is doing a research about other hotels in the area. So he starts searching hotels in his area, he is open to learn with their reviews and services. He is searching for a way to improve his own hotel.
  • Summer is coming and it will increase the number of people searching for hotels to stay. he needs to know which day would be the better day to announce his new pools and cinema room.

Persona 2:

Persona:

Restaurant lover in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Fictional Name:

Barbara Gordon

Job Title:

Subject Matter Expert

Demographics:

  • 22 years old
  • Single
  • No children, but 4 younger siblings
  • Her hobby is to visit new restaurants to try new food every week.

Goals and Tasks:

She loves to try new things and go out every week. As an SME she works really hard, so she likes to enjoy her day offs.
She enjoys:

  • Going to the movies
  • Try foods from different cultures and countries, even if it's at a local restaurant
  • Playing with her siblings (though she can't do it as much as she would like to)

Environment:

She knows a lot about computers due her profession, but she doesn't have a lot of time to spend in different websites. She is looking for something that would be easy and safe to find new restaurants to go.

Quote:

"I work a lot, so I deserve to eat something good!"

Scenarios:

  • She wants her dinner to be somewhere outside this time, she needs to check what are the options for restaurants that have external areas to eat.
  • Barbara's mother can't prepare her 4 siblings lunch, so she needs to find a restaurant to take them. She is searching for restaurants with good reviews, that have healthy food and a space for children to play while wait for food.
  • She is planning a date and her friend said he always wanted to try Chinese food. In Rio is hard to know where she can find this kind of restaurant and the date is tonight. She is looking for a Chinese restaurant which she can make the reservations as soon as possible and without complications.
  • After a hard and tiring day at her job, Barbara wants to eat at the new restaurant that opened downtown. She doesn't know what is its open time, so she needs to find good information about that.
  • Barbara is tired to eat at the same restaurants in her area. She wants to know which restaurants are new in her region, so she uses a filter to find just the brand new ones. Her preference was for Brazilian food restaurants.
